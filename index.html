Simone Raponi

I am currently a Machine Learning Scientist at NATO STO Center for Maritime and Experimentation. I am advised by Paolo Braca and Leonardo Millefiori. My major research interests include Artificial Intelligence, Cybersecurity, and Privacy.

I received both my Bachelor and Master's Degree (with honors) in Computer Science at Sapienza, University of Rome, advised from Francesco Parisi Presicce and Julinda Stefa, respectively, and a Ph. D. in Computer Science and Engineering at Hamad Bin Khalifa University in Doha, Qatar, advised by Roberto Di Pietro and co-advised by Gabriele Oligeri.
